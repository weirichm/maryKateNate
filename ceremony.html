The Ceremony

Jones Chapel
at Meredith College

Saturday The Twenty Third of June

two thousand and eighteen

at half past five o'clock in the evening


Parking:

There is a lot designated for the ceremony next to The Chapel. Drive around the fountain and make a Right turn into the lot. Please know that it is available for the ceremony only, please DO NOT leave your car here during the reception. Additional parking will also be available on the surrounding streets.

Reception to follow immediately after The Ceremony!
